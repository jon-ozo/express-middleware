ABOUT THE AUTHOR


Amb. Godwin Onyebuchi Ozor (DIL, BSc, MSc)

Is a Political Scientist and Public Administrator with a special interest in political economy, particularly the free market economy. He is a business tycoon, an entrepreneur, a family man, a counselor, a teacher, a philanthropist, and also an influential politician. His laudable work as the Chairman of Advocacy for Youth Empowerment and Emancipation, a non-governmental organization into the empowerment of oppressed youths who may have lost their way. This very deed of youth guidance and empowerment earned him the prestigious Ambassadorial award from the ICOSD – a reputable international organization for sustainable development and poverty eradication. Amb. Godwin has a huge passion for youth development and empowerment. He believes that investment in human infrastructure is key to the growth and all-around development of the human race. He is a very influential writer with lots of books to his name. Books like: “Don’t near the fresh water, the educated will swim first”, and “Infrastructure for Prosperity” among others. Not forgetting his latest and best-selling book, “Cometh the man Cometh the hour.”

BOOK DESCRIPTION

The book ‘Cometh the man, Cometh the hour’ is designed to make Nigerians deeply familiar with the personality, policies and programmes of His Excellency Mr. Peter Obi for the forth coming 2022 General Elections. His strategies, ideas & policies on free market economy, prudent management of resources, cutting costs of governance, investment in agriculture, physical and Human Infrastructure etc are portrayed in the book
